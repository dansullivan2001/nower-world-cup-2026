Course: A 60-minute score event for experienced orienteers, with a World Cup 2026 theme. There are 31 controls on the course — visit them in any order within the time limit. Controls are worth different amounts depending on the round, and knockout controls must be earned before they score.

Controls are organised into four group-stage sets and a knockout bracket:

Group A — The Americas Derby11, 12, 13, 14, 15, 1610 pts

Final9950 pts

Maximum possible score: 430 points.

Knockout progression rules: A quarter-final only scores if you have punched 3 or more controls from its parent group. A semi-final only scores if both quarter-finals in that half have been scored. The Final only scores if both semi-finals have been scored. Punching a locked control records the visit but scores zero — no honour system needed, the scoring is applied automatically after the event.

Time penalty: −10 points per minute (or part thereof) over 60 minutes. A finish at 61:30 incurs a −20 point penalty. Plan your route to get back on time — a minute late costs you a quarter-final.

Starts: 18:30 to 19:30. Course closes 20:30.

Format: exclusively on the MapRun app (version 7) — the app 'beeps' when you visit a control. No physical controls or dibbers. You can also use the MapRunG app on a compatible Garmin watch. Details of how orienteering works with this app are here. Download the course before you arrive: tap 'Select Event' and follow the menu path UK > Mole Valley > 2026 Summer Series.
