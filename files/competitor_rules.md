## The Format

This is a **60-minute score event**. Visit as many controls as you like, in any order, within the time limit. Your score is the total points from valid controls, minus any time penalty for a late finish.

**31 controls are out on the course**, but not all of them are worth the same — and some must be *earned* before they count.

## Progression Rules — How to Unlock the Knockout Rounds

The knockout controls (19, 29, 39, 49, 59, 69, 99) only score points if you have **qualified** for them. Punching a locked control scores **zero** — the GPS records the visit, but the points don't count.

### Quarter-finals

Each quarter-final is linked to its parent group:

| Quarter-final | Unlocked by |
|---|---|
| QF-A (control 19) | Punching **3 or more** controls from Group A (11–16) |
| QF-B (control 29) | Punching **3 or more** controls from Group B (21–26) |
| QF-C (control 39) | Punching **3 or more** controls from Group C (31–36) |
| QF-D (control 49) | Punching **3 or more** controls from Group D (41–46) |

### Semi-finals

| Semi-final | Unlocked by |
|---|---|
| SF1 (control 59) | **Both** QF-A (19) **and** QF-B (29) scored |
| SF2 (control 69) | **Both** QF-C (39) **and** QF-D (49) scored |

### Final

| Control | Unlocked by |
|---|---|
| Final (control 99) | **Both** SF1 (59) **and** SF2 (69) scored |

> **Note:** "Scored" means the control was both punched **and** unlocked. Punching a locked quarter-final does not count towards a semi-final.

## Time Penalty

You have **60 minutes** from your start time.

- Finishing within 60 minutes: no penalty
- Finishing late: **−10 points per minute (or part thereof)** over the limit

A runner finishing at 61:30 incurs a **−20 point** penalty (90 seconds late = 2 started minutes × 10).

Plan your route to get back on time — a late finish can wipe out an entire quarter-final.

---

## Strategy Notes

- The group stage is where most points are made. You don't need to collect all six from a group — just three to qualify — but all six add up.
- A balanced route through all four groups is likely to outscore a deep dive into just one or two.
- The Final (99) is worth 50 points, but requires punching and qualifying through eight earlier controls. It rewards the runner who covers the most ground efficiently.
- Going late is almost never worth it. At 10 points per started minute, just over a minute late costs you a quarter-final.

## How It Works on the Day

1. **Register and collect your map** at the start area.
2. **Start when ready** between 18:30 and 19:30 — your personal 60-minute clock begins when you start the MapRun course on your phone.
3. **Navigate to controls** using the map. Punch each one using the MapRun app on your phone (GPS-based — no physical punch needed).
4. **Return to the finish** before your 60 minutes are up.
5. **Results** will be calculated after the event using a custom scoring system that applies the World Cup progression rules. The MapRun app will show a raw score, but the official results may differ once progression rules are applied.
